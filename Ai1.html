Applications of Artificial Intelligence

Exploring how AI is shaping our world

Healthcare

AI helps doctors diagnose diseases, analyze medical images, and even assist in drug discovery.

Education

Smart tutors, personalized learning systems, and automated grading make learning more efficient.

Finance

AI powers fraud detection, stock market predictions, and customer support chatbots.

Transportation

Self-driving cars, traffic prediction, and smart logistics rely heavily on AI systems.

Robotics

AI-driven robots assist in manufacturing, space exploration, and even household chores.

Entertainment

Streaming platforms use AI for recommendations, gaming NPCs, and content creation.
